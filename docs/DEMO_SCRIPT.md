# Demonstration Walkthrough Script - LifeBridge AI

This document provides a script for presenters demonstrating LifeBridge AI.

---

## Part 1: Setting the Scene
"Meet John, a 65-year-old managing type-2 diabetes in rural Ohio. John's caregiver uses LifeBridge AI to manage his daily medication schedules and find low-cost clinical follow-ups."

---

## Part 2: Running the Chat Session
1. **Action**: Open the LifeBridge AI Guide chat widget.
2. **Action**: Click the demo button: *"My father is diabetic and forgets his Metformin daily."*
3. **Dialogue**: "Observe how the Master Orchestrator instantly identifies the intent. It schedules Medication alerts, flags consult parameters, searches for Mercy clinic options, and runs a Reflection safety audit before displaying the unified schedule advice."

---

## Part 3: Dashboard Compliance
1. **Action**: Navigate to the **Medicine Tracker** tab.
2. **Action**: Click the **Check** icon next to Metformin to log a dose as Taken.
3. **Dialogue**: "John's caregiver logs a taken dose, and the Adherence chart instantly updates to 88.5%, showing compliance telemetry."

---

## Part 4: Emergency Bypass
1. **Action**: Input: *"My father is having chest pain right now."*
2. **Dialogue**: "In a critical situation, standard planning routines are bypassed. The interface flashes red, showing first aid steps and loading 24/7 ER coordinates at County General Hospital."
