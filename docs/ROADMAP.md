# Product Roadmap - LifeBridge AI

This document outlines the milestones and roadmap for future iterations of LifeBridge AI.

---

## Milestone 1 (Current Version) — Core Multi-Agent Base
- Multi-agent orchestration using Gemini 2.5 Flash.
- Unified glassmorphic patient dashboard.
- Safe clinical reflection, emergency bypass, and low-cost clinic navigation.

---

## Milestone 2 (v2.0) — Wearable Integrations & Telemedicine
- **Smartwatch/Wearable Sync**: Import real-time heartbeat and step metrics to check blood pressure correlations.
- **ECG Sensor Parsing**: Multimodal ECG waveform image checking for automated arrhythmia flags.
- **Rural Telemedicine Call Hub**: Direct dialing integration to volunteer healthcare clinics.

---

## Milestone 3 (v3.0) — National Healthcare Integrations
- Direct FHIR API integrations to sync medical records with local EHR systems.
- Automated regional coverage scheme mappings for public health clinics.
