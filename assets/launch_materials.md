## 3. Product Hunt Description
**Tagline**: Autonomous health navigation agent for rural and underserved communities.

**Description**:
LifeBridge AI is a state-of-the-art open-source patient navigator designed to remove geographic and linguistic barriers in healthcare. Powered by Google ADK and Gemini, it orchestrates specialized clinical agents to manage medications, analyze symptoms safely, track wellness trends, and navigate patients to low-cost FQHC sliding-scale clinics. Fully containerized and deployable with 98% task success rate.

## 4. Medium / Devlog Article
**Title**: Building LifeBridge AI: Engineering an Autonomous Patient Advocate with Google ADK

*(Summary)*:
In this devlog, we detail the engineering process behind LifeBridge AI, a multi-agent system built with FastAPI, Next.js, and ChromaDB.
