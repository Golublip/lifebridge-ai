# Video Editing Guidelines - LifeBridge AI

- **Music**: Warm acoustic corporate or light tech ambient (non-copyright). Keep volume low (-20dB) during voiceover narration.
- **Transitions**: Smooth slide-left between dashboard tabs. High-speed cross-dissolves for B-Roll clips.
- **Color Grading**: Enhance the dark theme workspace. Boost the glowing blue/cyan highlights, and ensure the red Emergency Mode looks intense but legible.
- **Text Overlays**: Use Outfit or DM Sans font for captions/text headers. Position text in the lower third using white fill with a thin dark drop shadow for maximum readability.
- **Pacing**: Fast-paced, engaging, under 3 minutes total length.
