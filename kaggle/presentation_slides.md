# LifeBridge AI - Pitch Deck & Presentation Slides

## Slide 1: Front Page
- **Title**: LifeBridge AI: Autonomous Healthcare Navigation
- **Subtitle**: Closing the gap in healthcare literacy and access for underserved communities.
- **Presenter**: Gauhar Ali

## Slide 2: The Healthcare Gap
- **The Problem**: Over 1 billion people live in rural areas with critical doctor shortages.
- **The Cost**: Unnecessary travel expenses, missed medications, and lack of safety triage.
- **The Solution**: An autonomous clinical navigation assistant that sits in your pocket.

## Slide 3: Multi-Agent Architecture
- **Master Orchestrator**: Uses Gemini 2.5 Flash to automatically plan and route clinical requests.
- **Clinical Specialists**: Symptom Analyzer, Med Tracker, Hospital Navigator, Nutritionist, Emergency Responder.
- **Safety Reflection**: Dedicated critique agent that intercepts and reviews proposed summaries for diagnoses or allergy violations.

## Slide 4: Unified Memory System
- **Short-Term**: Local session history for chat context.
- **Long-Term**: Persistent user profile database tracking allergies, conditions, and ZIP code.
- **Vector memory**: ChromaDB storage of historical prescription scans and lab reports.

## Slide 5: Social Good & Scalability
- **Health Equity**: Translations in Spanish, Swahili, and Hindi. Meal plans optimized for low budgets.
- **Roadmap**: ECG visual scans, smartwatch telemetry sync, and direct EHR connection.
